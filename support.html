FAQ

Does IntroCard require an account?

No. The app is designed to work without creating an account.

How does someone save my contact details?

Your IntroCard profile includes a vCard QR code. Anyone can scan it with their phone camera to save your contact information — they do not need the app.

Can I order physical business cards?

Yes. IntroCard generates a print-ready card image with your details on one side and your QR code on the other, which you can send to any printing service.

Can I add my photo?

Yes. IntroCard is built around a portrait photo alongside your name, title, and company details.
