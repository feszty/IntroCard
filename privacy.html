Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

Overview

IntroCard helps you create a digital business card in a simple and privacy-minded way. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

IntroCard does not require an account. The app is designed to build your card from information you enter into the app, such as your name, job title, company, contact details, and a portrait photo, and to generate a vCard QR code and a printable card image from those details.

Unless a future version clearly states otherwise, this information is processed locally on your device for the purpose of creating your card, QR code, and card image.

Personal Information

IntroCard does not sell personal information. The app does not intentionally collect personal information for advertising or tracking purposes. The contact details and photo you add are used to create your own business card and are shared only when you choose to share your card or QR code.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Apple may provide developers with anonymous crash reports, performance information, and basic App Store analytics. This information helps improve the app and does not personally identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

The app may use Apple platform services required for normal iOS operation. The website is hosted as a static site and does not require you to create an account.

Children's Privacy

IntroCard is not intended to collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@introcard.app.
