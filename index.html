IntroCard brings your professional identity together — a striking profile page, an instant QR code, and a card design ready for the printer.

Personal profile page

A clean, elegant page with your portrait photo, name, title, company, and contact details — your business card, on screen.

Scannable vCard QR

Every profile includes a vCard QR code. Anyone can scan it to save your full contact details straight into their phone — no app required on their end.

Print-ready card design

IntroCard generates a standard business card image — your info on one side, your QR code on the other — ready to send to any printer to order physical cards.
